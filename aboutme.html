Hey !

Welcome to my website! I am Quentin, a PhD student in Neuroscience at Lausanne University Hospital (CHUV) with a strong background in engineering and life sciences. My passion for neurosciences and artificial intelligence drives my desire to contribute to innovative projects, particularly in the field of neuro-engineering. I'm also passionate about space, astronautics and the subject of space medicine. Currently, I am working in the Microstructure Mapping Lab under the supervision of Ileana Jelescu. My education includes a MSc in Aerospace Engineering from ISAE-SUPAERO and another MSc in Life Sciences Engineering from EPFL.

In my free time, I enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking and diving (I hold PADI Advanced and Deep Diver certifications) and have also previously practiced skydiving (holding a B license). I'm also a big fan of game nights with friends or family. In addition to my PhD studies, I have also developed several projects that combine neurosciences, 3D printing, and Brain-Computer Interface. Please feel free to explore these projects further on my website.

If you have any opportunities, like PhD internships, collaborations, or challenges that align with my interests and goals, please don't hesitate to reach out and connect! I am always seeking new opportunities to further my personal and professional growth.
